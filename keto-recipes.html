Keto Celery & Cream Cheese Bites

A quick and satisfying low-carb snack.

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tbsp chives, chopped
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 4 celery stalks, chopped into 3-inch pieces
  • 1 tbsp walnuts, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix cream cheese, chives, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Fill celery sticks with cream cheese mixture.
  3. Sprinkly crushed walnuts on top if using.
  4. Enjoy immediately or refrigerate for later.
